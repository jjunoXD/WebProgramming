Missing Person Detection and Recognition in XR

Developed a real-time facial recognition system within an XR environment using YOLOv8n-face and MobileFaceNet to assist in locating missing individuals. Integrated the Unity Inference Engine with Meta Quest, and incorporated real missing person data through the OpenAPI provided by the Korean National Police Agency.
